Information We Collect

We collect different types of information in order to provide and improve our service to you. The data we collect includes:

Personal Information

  • Location Permission: You will be prompted to allow location permissions when using the app. You can choose to enable or disable this feature through your device’s settings at any time.

Usage Data

  • App Usage Information: We collect information about how you interact with the app, including features you use, time spent in the app, and technical data such as your device type and operating system.
  • Search History: If enabled, we may collect information about the destinations you search for and set as alerts. This allows you to quickly access recent locations.

Other Information

  • Device Information: Information such as your device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers may be collected automatically.
  • App Settings: Settings such as vibration alerts, notification preferences, and chosen units for distance (e.g., kilometers or meters) are stored to personalize your experience.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for various purposes, including:

  • Providing the Service: To offer accurate location-based notifications and alerts when you are approaching your destination.
  • Improving the App: To enhance the user experience, fix bugs, and optimize features based on your usage patterns.
