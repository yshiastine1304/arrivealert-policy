Privacy Policy for ArriveAlert

At ArriveAlert, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This policy outlines the data we collect, how we use it, and how you can request deletion.

User Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your personal data from our systems, please follow the instructions below:

  1. Send an email to support@arrivealert.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include your registered email address and specify that you wish to delete your data.
  3. Your request will be processed within 30 days, and you will receive a confirmation email.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at support@arrivealert.com.
